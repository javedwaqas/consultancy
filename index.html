About

We help our clients unlock their business potential by leveraging latest technologies and improving underlying processes. We look at novel technologies as a means to bring positive change in people's lives and the World at large. We believe businesses can only thrive when they positively impact the quality of life of their customers continuously, no matter how small or large. For this, we work with our clients to discover opportunities, envision new processes, and implement technology transformation to continuously improve their business impact on their customers' lives.

Services

Our portfolio of services includes pre-defined offerings as well as customized solutions to meet the needs of our clients' business. Following provides an overview of our services.

Business Intelligence

Conducted a week long discovery workshop to map out end to end processes and challenges faced by teams across 5 different departments. For each department, created journey maps to identify dependencies across departments and pain points faced during day to day tasks. Outcomes of the workshop included; i) process improvement opportunities, ii) opportunities priority matrix based on value vs effort, and iii) notional road map for solutions implementation.
